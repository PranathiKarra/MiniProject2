Best Season to Visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus

October to March is the Best Season to Visit Goa

The most pleasant time to visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus in Goa is between October and March. The weather during this season is cooler and more comfortable, perfect for sightseeing and exploring Old Goa's historic churches. December is especially popular due to the Feast of St. Francis Xavier on December 3rd, drawing pilgrims and tourists from around the world.

Ways to Reach the Basilica of Bom Jesus

By Air

The nearest airport is Goa International Airport (Dabolim), located around 25 km from the Basilica. From the airport, taxis, rental cars, and app-based ride services are available to Old Goa where the basilica is located.

By Rail

The nearest railway station is Karmali (about 3 km away), followed by Madgaon (35 km) and Vasco da Gama (29 km). You can take local taxis or autos to reach the basilica from the station.

Book Train Tickets to Goa

By Road

Goa has a well-connected road network. You can reach Old Goa by buses run by Kadamba Transport Corporation, private buses, or taxis from Panaji (10 km) and other parts of the state. Renting a scooter or car is also a popular option for tourists.
