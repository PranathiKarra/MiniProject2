Best Season to visit Kanchi

October to March is the best season to visit Kanchi.

Kanchipuram, home to the sacred Kamakshi Amman Temple, can be visited year-round, but the best time to explore this spiritual city is during the winter months from November to February. The weather during this period is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for temple visits, leisurely walks through the temple corridors, and exploring the rich architectural heritage of Kanchipuram.

Ways to reach Kanchi

By Air

The city of Kanchipuram, where the revered Kamakshi Amman Temple is located, is served by Chennai International Airport, situated about 70 km away. The airport is well-connected to major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and international destinations.

By Rail

Kanchipuram is well-connected by rail through the Kanchipuram Railway Station, located close to the city center and just a few kilometers from the Kamakshi Amman Temple. The station has regular train services to and from major cities in Tamil Nadu like Chennai, Chengalpattu, Tirupati, and Bangalore.

By Road

When it comes to reaching Kanchipuram by road, the city is well-connected to major towns and cities in Tamil Nadu and neighboring states through a robust network of national and state highways. Regular intercity and interstate buses, operated by both government and private operators, run frequently to Kanchipuram from Chennai, Vellore, Tiruvannamalai, Bengaluru, and other nearby cities, making road travel to the Kamakshi Amman Temple convenient, affordable, and easily accessible.
