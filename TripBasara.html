Best Season to visit Madurai

October to March is Best Season to Visit Madurai

Madurai can be visited at any time. However, Winter from October to March is the best time to visit Madurai to explore its ancient temples and architectural beauty of the huge temple. This is the time the climate is cool and pleasant to travel through the city without any effects of heat. This is also the best time for sightseeing and other outdoor activities as the humidity of the city is less compared to summer.

Best Season to Visit Basara

October to March is the Best Season to Visit Basara

Basara can be visited throughout the year. However, the winter season from October to March is considered the best time to visit Basara. During this period, the weather is cool and pleasant—ideal for exploring the renowned Gnana Saraswati Temple and the peaceful Godavari riverbanks. The pleasant climate enhances the experience of temple visits and sightseeing without the discomfort of summer heat.

Ways to Reach Basara

By Air

The nearest airport to Basara is Rajiv Gandhi International Airport in Hyderabad, approximately 240 km away. It is well-connected to major cities across India such as Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Bengaluru. Taxis and buses are readily available from the airport to reach Basara comfortably.

Book Air Tickets to Hyderabad

By Rail

Basara has its own railway station—Basar Railway Station (BSX)—with regular train services from cities like Nizamabad, Secunderabad, and Nanded. Trains from Hyderabad and other major railway junctions make it easy to reach Basara directly.

Book Train Tickets to Basara

By Road

Basara is well connected by road to cities in Telangana and Maharashtra. Both government-run and private buses operate frequently from Hyderabad, Nizamabad, and Nanded. You can also drive down or hire a cab to enjoy a scenic road journey to Basara.
