Best Season to visit Trimbakeshwar

October to February is the Best Season to visit Trimbakeshwar.

Trimbakeshwar can be visited throughout the year. However, winter from October to February is the best time to visit Trimbakeshwar, especially to experience the spiritual and natural beauty of the temple town. During this season, the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for exploring the ancient Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga Temple, participating in rituals, and visiting nearby attractions like Kushavarta Teerth and Brahmagiri Hills. This is also the best time for sightseeing and outdoor activities, as the humidity and heat are significantly lower compared to the summer months.

Ways to reach Trimbakeshwar

By Air

The nearest airport is Nashik Airport (Ozar Airport), located about 30 km away. However, for better connectivity, most travelers prefer to fly into Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, which is approximately 180 km from Trimbakeshwar. Both airports are connected to major Indian cities like Delhi, Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Ahmedabad through regular domestic flights. From the airport, one can hire a taxi or cab to reach Trimbakeshwar comfortably.

By Rail

. The nearest major railway station is Nashik Road Railway Station, located about 40 km from Trimbakeshwar. Nashik Road Station is well-connected to major cities across India such as Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Kolkata, and Hyderabad through the Indian Railways network.

By Road

When it comes to reaching Trimbakeshwar through the road network, the town is well-connected by roads and national highways to major cities in Maharashtra and nearby states. It lies about 30 km from Nashik, and is easily accessible via the NH-848 and NH-60. Frequent intercity and interstate buses, both government (MSRTC) and private operators, run between Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, and Shirdi to Trimbakeshwar.
