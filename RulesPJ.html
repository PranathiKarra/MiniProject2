Tips and Rules for visiting the Temple


  • Wear traditional Indian clothing like Sarees or Half-Sarees for women and Dhoti or Pancha for men.

  • Leave your footwear and socks in your car or bus. You can’t wear it in the temple premises

  • Mobile phones, cameras, electronic items, sprays of any kind, hand bags, backpacks wrist watches, calculators etc. are not allowed. Remember to leave them behind. If you still happen to have them with you, you will be forced to leave them behind in a locker near the entrance for a nominal fee.

  • Entry into the temple is allowed for Hindus only.

  • Non-vegetarian or any other outside food is not allowed inside the temple premises.

  • Donations can be dropped into the donation boxes (Hundi)
