Golden Temple

#1 of 29 Places to Visit in Punjab

The Golden Temple in Amritsar, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest shrine of Sikhism. It is a beautiful marble temple overlaid with gold leaf and surrounded by the sacred Amrit Sarovar (Pool of Nectar). ...Thousands of pilgrims visit daily to offer prayers, take a dip in the holy water, and participate in the community Langar. Jallianwala Bagh

#2 of 29 Places to Visit in Amritsar

Located near the Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh is a historic public garden and memorial of national importance. It commemorates the tragic massacre of hundreds of Indians in 1919. ...Visitors can still see bullet marks on the walls and the preserved well into which people jumped to escape gunfire. It serves as a poignant reminder of India's struggle for freedom. read more
