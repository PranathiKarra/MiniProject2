Best Season to visit Srisailam

September to March is Best Season to Visit Srisailam

It is situated in the state of Andhra Pradesh, where summers are said to exceed limits and it becomes hot and humid. It is recommended that you visit Mallikarjuna Temple from September to March. The weather is pleasant for darshan and sightseeing.

Ways to reach Srisailam

By Air

The nearest airport to Srisailam is Kurnool Airport which is 180km from Srisailam. But it is a domestic airport and the nearest major airport is the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad which is just 217 km from the main city.

Book Air tickets to Srisailam

By Rail

Srisailam also has its own railway station, Markapuram Railway Station which is just 85 km from the main city. Srisailam is connected with most of the other major cities and states through Indian railway network. Taxi/cab are available from the railway station which you can hire to reach your destination in Srisailam.

Book Train tickets to Srisailam

By Road

When it comes to reaching Srisailam through road network, the city is pretty-well connected with other major southern states and cities through roads and national highways. From these states and cities, interstate or intercity buses, both government and private, are available to Srisailam which runs on a frequent basis.
