Best Season to visit Shirdi

October to February is Best Season to Visit Shirdi

Shirdi, Maharashtra, can be visited year-round, but the best time is from October to March, during the winter season. This is when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for temple visits, darshan, and exploring nearby attractions like Shani Shingnapur and Nashik. The humidity and heat are much lower compared to summer, allowing visitors to comfortably move around and enjoy the spiritual atmosphere. It's also the best time for sightseeing and outdoor activities without discomfort from high temperatures.

Ways to reach Shiridi

By Air

The town of Shirdi has its own airport—Shirdi International Airport (SAG)—located around 14 km from the main temple area. It is well-connected to major Indian cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, and Ahmedabad through daily domestic flights.

By Rail

Shirdi also has its own railway station—Sainagar Shirdi Railway Station (SNSI)—which is located just about 3 km from the Sai Baba Temple. Shirdi is well-connected to major cities like Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru through the Indian Railways network.

By Road

When it comes to reaching Shirdi via road, the town is well-connected to major cities in Maharashtra and neighboring states through a network of national and state highways. Cities like Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, and Ahmednagar offer frequent interstate and intercity buses, operated by both government (MSRTC) and private operators.
